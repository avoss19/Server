Project-Euler

This is a project that I began to attempt to solve all the problems on projecteuler.net and the project can be accessed at github.com/avoss19/Project-Euler. These problems are called Euler problems, which are problems that are challenging for a computer to solve. These problems require a lot of creativity to solve the problems within a timely manner because a computer can only do so many calculations per second. I started this project due to it sometimes being challenging for me to find a good way to spend my time. I find that solving these problems are more useful than me spending my time playing video games or watching Netflix. I feel that these problems have taught me to approach problems in a different manner and force me to take a more ingenious approach.
